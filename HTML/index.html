About Me

Hey You.

My name is Ryan Drake.

I am 21 years old but feel as if I’ve already lived through many lifetimes. I dropped out of university to pursue a career as a developer + designer. I like to create art with my fingers - typically on a keyboard, and usually with a mouse.

When I am not coding or playing around in Photoshop, you can find me at CrossFit 4000 smashing a ‘WOD’.

Beyond the gym, I love to write, read, travel the world and seek adventure in the everyday and mundane.

I am reminded of my favourite quote when I reflect upon my desire to live my best life:

Our greatest fear is not that we are inadequate. Our deepest fear is that we are powerful beyond measure. It is our light, not our darkness, that most frightens us.

This blog purely exists as an experiment of sorts to see what it takes to live a remarkable life. I desire to help others break through the mental limitations that so often hold us back from our dreams, to help people punch their fears in the face and to help inspire other trailblazers to forge their own paths and write the exciting next chapter of their lives.

Whilst I wish with all my heart that I could extend my hand to you through my screen and pull you in for a hug, some geographical limitations exist that make it difficult for me to do so. However, if you’re ever in Brisbane and wish to catch up for coffee, feel free to shoot me a note.
